Commercial Landscaping Services

Renovation and Installation

Our skilled installation crews bring your landscape designs to life, creating stunning flowerbeds, stonemasonry walls, patios, or walkways. We ensure minimal disruption, a clean worksite, and a picture-perfect finish.

Maintenance & Grounds Management

We provide comprehensive landscape maintenance solutions tailored to your business. From lawn care to tree and shrub management, we ensure your property remains immaculate while controlling costs.

Vegetation Management

We specialize in eliminating overgrown vegetation, weeds, or unwanted trees around your property. Our services help prevent damage to drainage lines, driveways, and sidewalks while keeping your property safe and clean.

Seasonal Lawn Care

Ensure your commercial property looks its best year-round with seasonal lawn care services tailored to maintain a professional and welcoming appearance.

Spring and Fall Cleanups

Our seasonal cleanups include debris removal, bed preparation, and general property tidying to keep your landscape fresh and inviting throughout the year.

Bush and Tree Trimming & Removal

We provide professional bush and tree trimming services to maintain your property’s safety, aesthetics, and functionality. Removal services are also available for problematic vegetation.

About Us

Family Oriented Run and Operated

FAIRWAY LANDSCAPE CONCEPTS, INC., was originally founded by Adrian Waasdorp in 2009 and was known as Fairway Mowing. In 2013, Adrian was joined by his father, Scott Waasdorp. The beginning of 2016 also marked the new beginning for the company, as son and father incorporated their business to the present entity. They guarantee that the backbone of the company’s goal has been and will always be, “Absolute customer satisfaction.”
