Residential Landscaping Services

Design: Our Process

We begin with an initial consultation, where we understand your aspirations. Using PRO Landscape Design Software, we create realistic designs that fit your budget, focusing on sunlight, privacy, and overall property characteristics.

Installation: Care, Respect, Quality

We treat your property with care and respect during every step of the installation process. With superior materials and expert workmanship, we deliver landscapes of lasting beauty and functionality.

Maintenance: For Years of Enjoyment

Our Landscape Asset Protection and Improvement Program offers seasonal lawn mowing, cleanups, edging, mulching, aeration, fertilization, and more to keep your property impeccable all year.

Outdoor Living Spaces

Transform your backyard with patios, pergolas, seating areas, outdoor kitchens, fire pits, and more. We create functional outdoor spaces tailored to your lifestyle.

Specialty Services

Our specialties include patios, retaining walls, landscape lighting, tree and bush removal, drainage management, and flower or shrub installations.

Complete Lawn Care

From lawn mowing, fertilization, and over-seeding to bed preparation, edging, and mulching, we offer services to keep your landscape healthy and vibrant.

About Us

Family Oriented Run and Operated

FAIRWAY LANDSCAPE CONCEPTS, INC., was originally founded by Adrian Waasdorp in 2009 and was known as Fairway Mowing. In 2013, Adrian was joined by his father, Scott Waasdorp. The beginning of 2016 also marked the new beginning for the company, as son and father incorporated their business to the present entity. They guarantee that the backbone of the company’s goal has been and will always be, “Absolute customer satisfaction.”
